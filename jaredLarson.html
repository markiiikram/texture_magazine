Centrism

by Jared Larson

Jared Larsen works with the photographic image, but is rarely a photographer. In the past his projects have dealt with the ideas of memory, photo as document and family history. He tries to explore the many ways in which the photographic image — despite its ubiquity in consumerism — can remain a vestige for the personal or private. He was educated slightly, and works with a collective called The Straw that calls Calgary, AB home. He also has interest in illustration.

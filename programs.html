Programs

The Montreal Institute of Classical Homeopathy offers:

  • 1. A Certificate in Homeopathy - A three year training in homeopathy.
  • 2. A full Diploma in Homeopathy (D.Hom.) – For those who have completed the three-year Certificate training in homeopathy and who have the medical science and other required complementary courses.

Option 1: Certificate Program in Homeopathy

This is the three-year training offered by MICH (see below for the content of the program).

For those who already have a practice and who simply want to add homeopathy as a technique to their practice, the Certificate Program is an excellent option.

Option 2: Full Diploma Program in Homeopathy (D.Hom.)

The D.Hom designation, allows you to be recognized provincially (Quebec), nationally, and internationally as a practitioner of homeopathy. Although MICH does not offer the complementary courses, it can accredit these courses when taken elsewhere, if they meet the requirements (see below). For those who do not have previous training or a Bachelors degree MICH can make suggestions to various institutions for in-class or on-line courses that are accredited. It is preferred that these courses are completed before entrance into the MICH Program, but exceptions can be made.

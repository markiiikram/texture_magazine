Cold Brew

by Caitlin Thompson

Cold Brew is a visual narrative about tea, beards, love, ill-fated love, murder, ships, ship wrecks, sea monsters, sea cows, stilts, yaks, fur, yurts, birthing, frozen lakes, ice and snow. Here's a short exerpt in which Assam Iam is poisoned by Dartmouth Dragontears and Darjeeling Clementine turns the ship into a funeral pyre.

Specs: 44 pages, black and white, 8.5" x 5.5", $10

Cold Brew is on sale at Drawn & Quarterly, Monastiraki and starting June 19 at La Centrale

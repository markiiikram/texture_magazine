New Works (part 2 of 2)

by Mandi Morgan

Mandi Morgan's artwork is motivated by impulse rather than premeditated conception. Her pieces develop rich and allegorical meaning as the creative process unfolds. While maintaining a sense of humanism and naivety in the subjects themselves, their surroundings delicately disclose universal concerns such as the environment, survival and greed.

Mandi Morgan's sound influences are Stars of the Lid, Tor Lundvall and Casino Versus Japan. Her illustrative influences are Gustav Klimt, Aubrey Beardsley and Hieronymus Bosch.
